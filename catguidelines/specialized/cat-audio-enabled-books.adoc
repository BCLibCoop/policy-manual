Audio-Enabled Books
~~~~~~~~~
Audio-enabled books include such things as Wonderbook and Vox.

When cataloguing audio-enabled books, such as Wonderbooks and Vox, follow
these paramenters to ensure your items display as expected.
 
* Leader position 06 (Type) = a OR t
* Leader position 07 (Bib Level) = a, c, d, or m
* 008 position 23 (Form) = blank
* 336 (Content Type) = $aspoken word$bspw$2rdacontent. OR 337 (Media Type) = $aaudio$bs$2rdamedia.
* 336 (Content Type) is NOT $astill image$bsti$2rdacontent. 
* There is no 006 (Fixed-Length Data Elements - Additional Material Characteristics) in the MARC record.

When these parameters are met a record will display with both the _Book_ and _Preloaded Audio_ icons.
